## Explanation of permissions requested in the app

The list of permissions required are:

**uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"**
* This permission allows the application to open network sockets, connect to remote servers, and send or receive data over the internet. YGO! Card Search uses this permission to connect to Yu-Gi-Oh API.
